BEM widgets for a website accessible to people with disabilities

(Smooth Scrolling Sticky ScrollSpy Navigation)

Introduction

The following work was created in order to develop and implement several effective widgets / components that can be placed on websites, created strictly according to the rules of the BEM methodology and in accordance with the WAI-ARIA standard. The widgets themselves are also useful and functional elements of the website (responsive menu, expandable section blocks, link tiles-elements), easy to integrate also in the implementation of other projects, while maintaining a good visual side (aesthetics and functionality). Based on these criteria, the idea of combining the assumptions of the BEM methodology with the attributes of the WAI-ARIA standard is implemented.
